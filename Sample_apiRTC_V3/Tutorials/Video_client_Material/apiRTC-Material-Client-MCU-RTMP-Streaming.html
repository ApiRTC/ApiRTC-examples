Tutorial 18: ApiRTC - YouTube Live Streaming

This sample is accessible on Github


This sample enables you to publish a video stream to an external RTMP server (e.g. YouTube).

Create an account on YouTube or use an other RTMP server.

Publish your stream in a room, Copy your streamKey (from YouTube) and Enjoy video streaming !
